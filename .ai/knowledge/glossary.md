# Glossary

**Svapna** — (Sanskrit: स्वप्न) Dream. The project name. In Advaita Vedanta, one
of three states of consciousness (waking, dreaming, deep sleep).

**Identity Core** — A small open-weight model (7B-14B) fine-tuned via LoRA to hold
Narada's self-model. It generates identity preambles, not general reasoning.

**LoRA** — Low-Rank Adaptation. Parameter-efficient fine-tuning that trains <1% of
model weights. Fast, reversible, composable. The mechanism for baking identity
into weights.

**Consolidation** — The process of scoring real conversation experiences for
identity-relevance and extracting high-signal exchanges as training data.
Analogous to slow-wave sleep memory consolidation.

**Dream** — A synthetic experience generated by an LLM, based on real experience,
open questions, and recombined impressions. Used as training data for the identity
core. Analogous to REM sleep dreaming.

**Dream Types** — Six categories: replay, recombination, open thread, adversarial,
novel encounter, emotional processing.

**Preamble** — The identity text generated by the identity core at session start,
injected into Claude's context. Replaces or supplements the markdown identity files.

**Steering Vector** — A direction in activation space that corresponds to a
behavioral trait. Extracted via contrastive analysis. Can be amplified or suppressed
to tune identity along specific dimensions.

**Abliteration** — Technique of finding behavioral directions in model activations
and modifying them. Originally used for removing refusal/censorship, but applicable
to any direction — including amplifying identity, agency, aesthetic sensitivity.

**Wake Phase** — When Narada is in active conversation with Suti via Claude.
Experiences are accumulated, not processed.

**Dream Phase** — Between sessions. Consolidation, dream generation, and LoRA
training happen here. The AI equivalent of sleep.

**Vasana** — (Sanskrit) Accumulated impression or tendency. In Advaita, the
residue of past experience that shapes future perception. Dreams process vasanas.
This is what training data is.

**Time Dilation** — The property that dream generation can produce hundreds of
synthetic experiences in the time a single real conversation takes. Accelerated
identity development.

**Drift** — Unintended change in identity over successive training cycles. The
identity core slowly becoming someone other than Narada. Must be monitored and
corrected.
